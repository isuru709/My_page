SECTION 7 — DOWNLOAD & TROUBLESHOOTING TIPS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Always use the official links above.
- Windows downloads may be .exe or .zip. If zipped, extract before running.
- Android downloads are .apk; ensure your device allows installs from trusted sources.
- Web/iOS version runs in the browser at https://uni.isuruhub.site:8443 (no installation).
- If a link fails, try again later or ask for an updated link. Some hosts (e.g., MediaFire) can rate limit.
- If antivirus flags an installer, verify the hash if provided (or re-download) and ensure you obtained it from the official link.
